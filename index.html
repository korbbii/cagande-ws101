Recipe

Filipino recipe for Chicken Adobo.

Adobo is a savory and slightly tangy dish, and it's a staple in Filipino cuisine.

This recipe serves 4.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg(3.3 lbs) chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 onion, peeled and sliced thinly
  • 1 head garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 cup water Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Marinate the Chicken:

In a large bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

2. Cooking the Adobo:

In a wide, heavy-bottomed pan, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Add sliced onions and saut√© until they become translucent

3. Add Marinated Chicken:

Add the marinated chicken to the pan. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the chicken is lightly browned

4. Pour Vinegar and Water:

Pour in the white vinegar and water. Allow it to boil without stirring. Simmer: Once boiling, lower the heat, cover the pan, and simmer for about 30 to 40 minutes or until the chicken is tender. Stir occasionally.

5. Seasoning:

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference. Serve: Serve the Chicken Adobo hot with steamed rice.
